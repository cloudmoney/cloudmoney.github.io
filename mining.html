What is mining



Mining is the process of creating new coins, either by verifying transactions on the blockchain network or by completing other computational tasks.

Mining requires hardware (GPU/CPU units) and software (available online).

By downloading the software from a pool-provider online and connecting your hardware, you are 'borrowing' your hash-rate (mining power) to the pool and when they are paid by the network, they provide you with your share of the earnings, depending on the amount of mining power you offered in the period.

Please be aware, pools often take a fee for maintenance and salaries

Why should i mine?



Mining is a fantastic way to get head first into the action and also to earn large portions of bitcoins ( or other coins!) directly to your wallet.

It does require a bit of a technical curiosity to research one self through the processes but it is possible nonetheless.

upsides

Healthy for the network

Can switch coin/pool at will

Vibrant sell-buy market for equipment

downsides

Expensive equipment

Reliant on cheap electricity

Requires physical space

Cloud Mining



Cloud mining is the easiest way to start mining right away! You can usually buy mining power with both fiat currency and crypto currency as soon as you have registered and are kept informed of the progress of your mining through an interactive interface with live-feed data.
