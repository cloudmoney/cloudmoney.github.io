Trading section

Perhaps the most profitable way of using your crypto currency, but also the riskiest, is trading. There are many ways of trading crypto currency. From the simplest 'buy-low-sell-high' strategy to the more advanced algorithmic approaches.

Trading is for everyone who finds it interesting, all you need is an account on some exchange and a few dollars to start out. However to keep growing those few dollars is an art.
Patience, clear thinking and research are your strongest tools.

Becoming a successful daytrader is not something that comes effortless. In order to maintain sharpness and keep profiting, one must stay in touch with all valuable sources of information, and handle this information correctly. Some people spend years pursuing this tempting life-style, not many succeeds. And those who do, rarely share their best tips and tricks.

Margin Trading

Margin trading is essentially trading with borrowed funds instead of your own. When you place a margin order, all of the money you are using is borrowed from other users offering their funds as peer-to-peer loans. The funds in your margin account are used only as collateral for these loans and to settle debts to lenders.
More information: poloniex, support

Exchange Trading

The simplest form of trading on an exchange-site, placing buy/sell orders with funds in your account. The one thing i like about exchange trading versus margin trading is the possibility of placing 'fishing orders' at a very low price, it doesnt cost you anything to have the order open, but if the price goes down you are already there with a buyorder to pick up some coins at a bargain price, only to place a sell order above what is currently visible on the orderbook.
